MindMosaic

An intelligent AI-based mental wellness assessment system that classifies emotional health into Low, Medium and High categories using structured ML logic.

Problem Statement

Students face increasing stress and anxiety. Most mental health tools rely on static questionnaires without intelligent scoring models. A structured ML-driven assessment is required.

Literature Review

Existing systems provide surveys or chatbots but lack normalized scoring and ML-based categorization.

Research Gap / Innovation

MindMosaic integrates XGBoost-based training, normalized scoring, and real-time classification visualization.

System Methodology

Dataset

25 structured psychological parameters.

Model

XGBoost Classification Model (Pickle Trained).
